The Importance of Core Learning at DevAcademy

As a student at Dev Academy, I have gained valuable insights about myself and my relationship with programming through the core learning component of the course.

Firstly, I have come to understand that coding can be humbling. Despite feeling confident in my abilities, I have encountered some aspects of programming that are particularly challenging. These difficulties have taught me to approach learning with more humility and patience.

Furthermore, I have learned the crucial roles of values, empathy, and self-awareness in the learning and programming process. By being aware of my own values, I am better equipped to collaborate with others and understand their perspectives. Empathy and self-awareness also help me to identify my own limitations and work towards improving them.

Another surprising aspect of the core learning was discovering that not all programming languages are equal in difficulty. I have found that some languages are more challenging for me, which has emphasized the importance of perseverance and determination in the learning process.

One of the most challenging aspects of the core learning has been the process of self-evaluation. It has been difficult for me to assess my own abilities and identify my strengths and weaknesses. However, this self-reflection has been critical in helping me to focus my efforts in the right areas and improve.

In conclusion, I believe that the focus on core learning at Dev Academy is essential for success in the field. By developing personal skills, we are better equipped to work in a coding environment and collaborate effectively with others. While the time spent on core learning may not feel like it could have been spent practicing coding, it has been a valuable investment in my personal growth and future success as a programmer.
